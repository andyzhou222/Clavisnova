Programs & Educational Schemes

Maintenance Training

Owning a piano comes with responsibility. For schools receiving a donation, we select and train a "Piano Guardian" to teach basic care, climate control, and simple troubleshooting.

  • Humidity & temperature regulation
  • Cleaning and key maintenance
  • Tuning appointment scheduling

Free Theory Workshops

Hardware is just the start. During school holidays, we offer free online and offline music theory workshops to help students understand the language of music more deeply.

  • Scheduled holiday sessions
  • Beginner to advanced levels
  • Completely free for partner schools

About Us

"Clavisnova" is a volunteer-led initiative founded by musicians and educators. We believe that high-quality instruments should not collect dust in corners while schools struggle for arts funding.

We found that while many families own quality pianos that are no longer used, many schools—especially in under-resourced areas—cannot afford even a basic upright piano. Our mission is to bridge this gap.

By facilitating the transfer of these instruments, we aren't just moving heavy wood; we are opening doors to creativity, discipline, and joy for thousands of students.
